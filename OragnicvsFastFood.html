Organic Food vs Fast Food

The importance of organic food

  • Organic food has more nutrients, for example vitamins and minerals.
  • Organic food has not been sprayed with so many chemicals and pestizides.
  • It is not that harmful to Planet Earth.
  • Buying organic food is an investment in a better future.
